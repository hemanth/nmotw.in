#[npm-whoshiring](https://www.npmjs.com/package/npm-whoshiring)
> npm who's hiring?

`npm-whoshiring` shows a table on your CLI of currently open jobs listed by npm partners.

__Get it:__ `npm i -g npm-whoshiring`
